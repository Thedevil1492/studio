# **App Name**: CosmicMind AI

## Core Features:

- Multimodal Life Scan: Accepts voice, text, photo, and links to gather user context for analysis.Explicit opt-in required before processing any user input.
- Personalized Insights Engine: Analyzes past and present data, leveraging psychology, astrology, numerology, and social footprint, to synthesize predictions and actionable solutions.
- Actionable Recommendations: Generates personalized, actionable solutions across career, health, relationships, finance, and spirituality domains.
- 3D Visualizer: Renders uploaded photos into 3D depth meshes with customizable auras, backgrounds, and outfit overlays.
- Social Media Analyzer: Analyzes social media links to suggest content, posting times, tone, and sample captions. Generates a viral score and A/B previews.
- OSINT connector: Collects publicly available information about the user from all their connected social networks.
- Ethical Moderation Tool: A tool that configures safety moderation with legal/compliance layers to ensure ethical use.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark mode with neon accents, inspired by the depths of space and the mysteries of the cosmos. The intent is to convey a sense of wonder, futurism, and introspection.
- Primary color: Vibrant purple (#A020F0) to represent spirituality, intuition, and the unknown.
- Background color: Dark desaturated purple (#1A0529) to create depth and focus.
- Accent color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF) for highlighting key elements and CTAs.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a tech-forward, modern feel. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for readability in longer text blocks.
- Minimalist icons with neon outlines to complement the dark mode aesthetic.
- Soft glassmorphism cards to provide depth and clarity while maintaining a futuristic look.
- Smooth micro-interactions and subtle animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
